## Critical Market Gaps

### 1. Nobody targets technical founders / developers who sell
Every product targets sales teams with dedicated SDRs/AEs. Zero products for indie hackers, technical founders, or developers pitching their own products.

### 2. Affordable real-time voice coaching doesn't exist
- Cogito does deep voice analysis → enterprise only (Fortune 25)
- Yoodli does voice feedback → practice only, not live
- Trellus does real-time → text-based, no voice analysis
- No product combines real-time voice tone/confidence analysis at individual pricing

### 3. Practice + Live coaching are siloed
- Roleplay tools (Hyperbound, Second Nature) don't do live coaching
- Real-time tools (Trellus, Aircover) don't do practice
- Almost nobody bridges practice → live performance

### 4. HOW you say it is underdeveloped
- Most tools focus on WHAT you say (talk tracks, methodologies)
- Almost none focus on HOW (confidence, energy, pace, tone) in real-time
- Cogito is the only serious voice behavioral analysis player, locked in enterprise

### 5. Pricing gap
- Roleplay: free tiers are limited, real value at $15K+/yr
- Real-time coaching: $95-150/user/mo minimum
- Nothing comprehensive under $20/mo for individuals
